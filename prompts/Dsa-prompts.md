## ⚡ Prompt Quality Guide

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| ✅ **Good Prompt** | Structured, deep explanation, interview-ready output |
| ❌ **Bad Prompt** | Vague, short, produces low-quality response |

> **Rule of Thumb:** The more context and constraints you give, the better the AI response.

## 🔥 Pro Tips

> **Daily Practice Formula:**
> ```
> 1 topic/day × 5 problems using these prompts = interview-ready in weeks
> ```

- Always start with the ✅ Good Prompt version
- Ask for **dry runs** — they reveal what you're missing
- After getting the solution, **code it yourself without looking**
- Review **time & space complexity** for every solution
- Revisit hard problems after 3 days (spaced repetition)
