## 🔥 Pro Tips

> **Daily Learning Formula:**
> ```
> 1 concept/day + 1 small code experiment = AI-proficient in months
> ```

- Always ask for **code + intuition** — not just theory
- Request **real-world analogies** for abstract concepts
- Ask the AI to **quiz you** at the end of each topic
- Build at least **one mini project** per major topic
- Study **failure cases** — understanding where models break teaches more than successes
